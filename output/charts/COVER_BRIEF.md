# Cover Design Brief

## Book Title
从零开始跑量化：11 堂课 × 30 年真实数据

## Subtitle (English)
Run Your First Quant Strategy: 11 Lessons × 30 Years of Real Data

## Design Concept

### Primary Visual
A stylized line chart that transforms mid-way from jagged, volatile lines (representing raw data) into a clean, upward-trending arrow (representing insight gained). The transition point features a small glowing "+44.5%" that dissolves into "+0.1%" — the book's central revelation.

### Color Palette
- Primary: Deep navy blue (#1a1a2e) — stability, trust
- Accent: Electric blue (#1971c2) — data, precision
- Highlight: Warm orange (#f08c00) — insight, revelation
- Warning: Deep red (#c92a2a) — the "vanished alpha"

### Typography
- Chinese title: Bold sans-serif (e.g., Source Han Sans, Noto Sans SC)
- English subtitle: Lighter weight, smaller size
- Code elements: Monospace (JetBrains Mono or Fira Code)

### Layout Options

**Option A: Minimalist**
- White background
- Single line chart spanning the middle third
- Title above, subtitle below
- Small "CC BY-NC-ND 4.0" badge bottom-right

**Option B: Dark Mode**
- Navy blue background
- White/gold chart with glowing elements
- Title in white, subtitle in light grey
- Dramatic, modern, tech-forward

**Option C: Notebook Style**
- Off-white background with subtle grid lines (like graph paper)
- Hand-drawn style chart (Excalidraw aesthetic)
- Title in marker-style bold font
- Warm, approachable, educational

## Logo Concept

The logo is a minimalist symbol combining:
- A stylized "Q" (for Quant)
- Inside the Q, a small upward arrow (representing growth/learning)
- Clean, geometric, recognizable at small sizes
